Course registration at Algonquin College is managed through the ACSIS system, which students can access using their college credentials. Full-time students are typically automatically enrolled in their required courses once tuition fees are paid, while part-time students must manually select and register for courses through the system.

Students are responsible for reviewing their course timetable to ensure all required courses are included and there are no conflicts. It is important to complete registration before the deadline to avoid losing access to courses or delays in starting the semester.

If a student wishes to change or drop a course, they must do so within the official add/drop period. After this deadline, changes may not be permitted or may result in academic or financial penalties.

Students experiencing issues with registration, such as missing courses or system errors, should contact the Registrar’s Office. Planning ahead and checking registration status early can help avoid last-minute problems and ensure a smooth start to the semester.
