Algonquin College offers a variety of student support services to help students succeed academically, professionally, and personally. These services include counseling, accessibility services, career support, and technical assistance.

Counseling services provide support for mental health, stress management, and personal challenges. Students can access confidential counseling sessions to help them cope with academic and personal pressures.

Accessibility services are available for students with disabilities. These services provide accommodations such as extended exam time, note-taking support, and assistive technologies.

Career services help students prepare for employment by offering resume reviews, interview preparation, and job search guidance. Workshops and career events are also available to connect students with potential employers.

Information Technology (IT) support assists students with login issues, account access, and software problems. Students can contact the Help Desk for technical assistance.

Using these support services can greatly enhance the student experience and improve overall success.
