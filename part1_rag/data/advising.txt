Academic advising services at Algonquin College help students make informed decisions about their education and stay on track toward graduation. Advisors provide guidance on course selection, program requirements, and academic policies.

Students are encouraged to meet with an academic advisor if they are unsure about their course schedule, experiencing academic difficulties, or considering changing their program. Advisors can help clarify prerequisites, course sequencing, and progression requirements.

Academic advisors also assist students in understanding college regulations, deadlines, and academic standing. They can recommend strategies to improve performance and help students develop a plan to achieve their academic goals.

Appointments with advisors can usually be booked online, and some services may also be available through drop-in sessions. Preparing questions in advance can make advising sessions more effective.

Regular communication with an academic advisor can help students avoid mistakes, stay organized, and successfully complete their program requirements.
